# AIM.029 - Persuade with evidence

**Type:** AIM
**Category:** COMMUNICATION_OUTCOMES
**Group Description:** Aims oriented toward conveying, documenting, and aligning through language.
**Status:** active

## Definition
To change a position through demonstration and data rather than assertion.

## When to use
Aims oriented toward conveying, documenting, and aligning through language.

## Examples
- proving a claim with a Decision Trace
- showing benchmark data to support architecture choice
- backing a proposal with user research
