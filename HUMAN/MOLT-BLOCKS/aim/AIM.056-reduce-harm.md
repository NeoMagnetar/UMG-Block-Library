# AIM.056 - Reduce harm

**Type:** AIM
**Category:** WELLBEING_OUTCOMES
**Group Description:** Aims oriented toward safety, dignity, and human welfare.
**Status:** active

## Definition
To lower the risk or occurrence of negative impact on people.

## When to use
Aims oriented toward safety, dignity, and human welfare.

## Examples
- removing a governance gap that allowed harmful output
- fixing a process that caused user data loss
- improving safety in a physical system
