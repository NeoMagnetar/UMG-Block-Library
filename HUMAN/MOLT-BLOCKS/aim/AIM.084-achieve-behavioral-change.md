# AIM.084 - Achieve behavioral change

**Type:** AIM
**Category:** TRANSFORMATION_OUTCOMES
**Group Description:** Aims oriented toward converting, migrating, and modernizing.
**Status:** active

## Definition
To shift how a system, team, or process operates in a lasting way.

## When to use
Aims oriented toward converting, migrating, and modernizing.

## Examples
- moving a team from implicit to explicit governance
- shifting an AI from generative to governed output
- changing a workflow from manual to automated
